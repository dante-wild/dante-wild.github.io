Method

Surface manifold unleashes volumetric hash encoding.

Clean features on the surface

UV-based featurizations can disorganize feature distribution, surface-based grids are limited by discretization, and volumetric featurizations disperse weights during volume rendering. Our method uses hash encoding with rasterization to propagate clean, multi-view consistent signals to surface features.

Cross-scale neural rendering

Hash featurized manifold better exploits unstructured capture viewpoints and scale variations, representing structural and textural details across different viewing scales.
